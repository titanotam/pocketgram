---
title: Present perfect
order: 2
---

The **present perfect** links past actions or states to the present moment.

## Form

*have/has* + past participle

:::example
I **have finished** the report.

She **has lived** here **since** 2010.

**Have** you **ever visited** Kyoto?
:::

## Common signals

- *already, yet, just, ever, never*
- *for* + period / *since* + point in time
- unstated time when the result still matters now
