---

The **present simple** describes habits, facts, and fixed arrangements.

## Form

- **Affirmative:** subject + base verb (+ *-s/-es* for he/she/it)
- **Negative:** subject + *do/does* + not + base verb
- **Question:** *Do/Does* + subject + base verb?

:::example
She **works** from home on Fridays.

**Do** they **live** nearby?

He **does not** (**doesn't**) **like** olives.
:::

## Uses

1. **Routines** — *I brush my teeth twice a day.*
2. **General truths** — *Water boils at 100°C.*
3. **Timetabled events** — *The train leaves at 6:12.*
