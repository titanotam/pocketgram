---
title: Modal verbs
order: 1
---

**Modal verbs** (*can, could, may, might, must, shall, should, will, would*) express ability, permission, obligation, probability, and more.

## Pattern

Modal + **base verb** (no *to* after the modal, except *ought to*).

:::example
You **must wear** a helmet.

We **could meet** at noon instead.

She **might be** late — traffic has been heavy.
:::

## Notes

- Modals have **no** *-s* on third person: *She can go.*
- Questions invert the modal and subject: *Should I call first?*
